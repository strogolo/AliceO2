5 # MCH Conditions
6 
7 ## DCS to CCDB
8 
9 To test the DCS to CCDB route you can use the following 3 parts worfklow pipeline :
10 
11 ```shell
12 o2-calibration-mch-dcs-sim-workflow --max-timeframes 600 --max-cycles-no-full-map 10 -b | \
13 o2-calibration-mch-dcs-processor-workflow --hv-max-size 0 --hv-max-duration 300 -b | \
14 o2-calibration-ccdb-populator-workflow --ccdb-path="http://localhost:6464" -b
15 ```
16 
17 - `o2-calibration-mch-dcs-sim-worfklow` is just generating fake random MCH DCS data points,
18 - `o2-calibration-mch-dcs-processor-workflow` gathers the received data points into a container object
19 - `o2-calibration-ccdb-populator-workflow` uploads the container object to the CCDB (in this example a local dev ccdb).
20 
21  The container object that groups the datapoints is considered ready to be shipped either when the data points span a long enough duration (see the `--hv-max-duration` and `--hv-max-duration` options of the `o2-calibration-mch-dcs-processor-workflow`) or is big enough (see the `--lv-max-size` and `--hv-max-size` options).

23 ## DCS Data Points
24 
25 ### HV
26 
27 The MCH high voltage (HV) system is composed of 188 channels :
28 
29 - 48 channels for stations 1 and 2 (3 HV channel per quadrant x 16 quadrants)
30 - 140 channels for stations 3, 4, 5 (1 HV channel per slat x 140 slats)
31 
32 ### LV
33 
34 The MCH low voltage (LV) system is composed of 328 channels :
35 
36 - 216 channels (108 x 2 different voltage values) to power up the front-end
37  electronics (dualsampas)
38 - 112 channels to power up the readout crates hosting the solar (readout) cards
39 
40 ## CCDB quick check
41 
42 Besides the web browsing of the CCDB, another quick check can be performed with
43  the `o2-mch-dcs-ccdb` program to dump the DCS datapoints (hv, lv, or both) or
44  the datapoint config valid at a given timestamp.
45 
46 ```
47 $ o2-mch-dcs-ccdb --help
48 $ o2-mch-dcs-ccdb --ccdb http://localhost:6464 --query hv --query lv --query dpconf
49 ```
50 
51 The same program can be used to upload to CCDB the DCS data point configuration
52  for the dcs-proxy :
53 
54 ```
55 $ o2-mch-dcs-ccdb --put-datapoint-config --ccdb http://localhost:8080
56 ```
